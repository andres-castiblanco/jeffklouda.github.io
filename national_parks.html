National Parks

Animated Event Promo

Description

The goal of this project was to create an animated promotion for some type of event. This is a concept for an imagined National Parks conference attended by the superintendents of major parks. My main focus in this piece was syncing its visuals to music and developing interesting transitions between scenes.

Tools

After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator
