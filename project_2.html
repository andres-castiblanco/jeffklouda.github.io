Mrs Eaves

Digital Type Poster

Description

The goal of this project was to create a static digital poster highlighting a web typeface. This poster features an interactive button which enables and disables a simple analysis over the letter K featured at the top of the page. The poster can be viewed at jeffklouda.com/typePoster.

Tools

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Illustrator

Initial Sketches

These are my initial sketches of the letterform analysis. I chose to analyze the K because it is nicely representative of the typeface as a whole with its serifs and stroke variation. These are the two elements of the typeface I tried to highlight in my analysis.
