Level 2

In this level, some of the notes are not on the beat. This is an eighth note. In this game that means it is one-half-beat long, so you can play two of them per beat!

Some of the notes in this level are between beats. Remember that each click of the metronome is one beat. Try to count “one, two, three, four, one, two three four” along with the metronome to get a feel for the beat!
