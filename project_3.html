Bauer Bodoni 4i

Letterform Composition

Description

The goal of this project was to combine 2 letterforms to create a visually interesting composition with positive and negative space. I created this composition of 4 and i in Bauer Bodoni Bold because of the way the serifs of the i interact with the 4â€™s serifs and negative space.

Tools

Illustrator

Initial Sketches

I experimented with several different characters and typefaces while developing this project. I found the most interesting compositions to be those in which both characters remain legible and the elements of the two characters align nicely.

Digital Sketches

These are the compositions I chose to refine digitally. I chose to pursue the 4i composition because it was the strongest and most legible composition of this set.
